Resources

Find resources, hotlines, and support for combating human trafficking in the UK.

UK-Specific Governmental and Law Enforcement Agencies

  • Gangmasters and Labour Abuse Authority (GLAA)
    Focus: Labour exploitation in agriculture, food processing, and construction sectors.
    Website: www.gla.gov.uk
  • National Crime Agency (NCA) - Modern Slavery Unit
    Focus: Investigating trafficking networks and modern slavery crimes.
    Website: www.nationalcrimeagency.gov.uk
  • Home Office - Modern Slavery Helpline
    Hotline: 08000 121 700
    Website: www.modernslaveryhelpline.org

UK-Based NGOs

  • Unseen UK
    Focus: Operates the Modern Slavery Helpline and provides training to prevent trafficking.
    Website: www.unseenuk.org
  • ECPAT UK
    Focus: Combating child trafficking, offering advocacy and research.
    Website: www.ecpat.org.uk
  • Anti-Slavery International
    Focus: Advocacy to address forced labour and domestic servitude.
    Website: www.antislavery.org
  • Hope for Justice
    Focus: Victim support and rescue services in the UK.
    Website: www.hopeforjustice.org

Collaborative UK Platforms

  • The Clewer Initiative
    Focus: Community awareness and intervention on modern slavery.
    Website: www.theclewerinitiative.org
  • Stop the Traffik UK
    Focus: Using data-driven insights to disrupt trafficking networks.
    Website: www.stopthetraffik.org
